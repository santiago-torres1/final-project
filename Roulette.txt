Waterloo's Casino Roulette instructions:
1. There are three colors: Black, red and green.
2. There are nineteen spots (Numbers 0-18)
3. Number 0 is green.
4. Even numbers are black, and odd numbers are red.
5. You can place a bet to any color.
6. After you place the bet, the roulette will spin.
7. If the ball lands on the same color of your bet, you win!

Payouts:
Black and Red pay 2x the bet (i. e. if you bet 100, you'll get 200).
Green pays 18x (i. e. if you bet 100, you'll get 1800).
